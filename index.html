— How It Works

From Hello to
Order Confirmed

The agent guides customers through a full ordering experience — no app, no menu, no friction.

01 — 👋
Warm Welcome

The barista greets the customer naturally and asks what they're in the mood for — warm, personal, on-brand.

02 — ☕
Drink Selection

Browse espresso drinks, cold brew, seasonal specials, and pastries. The agent clarifies size, milk, and temperature.

03 — 🧾
Live Pricing

Prices are fetched in real time from a Cloud Function — Dallas market rates, customization upcharges, and Texas sales tax included.

04 — ✅
Order Confirmed

The customer reviews a clean order summary, confirms, and receives a friendly wrap-up. Done in under a minute.

— Capabilities

Built for
Real Businesses

Not a toy. This is a production-grade conversational agent — the same architecture that powers white-label deployments for small business clients.

  • 🗣️

    Natural Language Understanding

    Dialogflow CX handles intent, entity extraction, and multi-turn context across the full ordering flow.

  • 💰

    Dynamic Pricing Webhook

    Live prices, upcharges, and tax calculated per order via a Node.js Cloud Function. No hardcoded values.

  • 🔌

    White-Label Ready

    Drop-in embed for any website. Configurable persona, menu, and branding per client deployment.
